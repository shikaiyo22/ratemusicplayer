Four ways to shuffle

'

FY — Fisher-Yates
Mathematical Shuffle
Pre-generates a complete random sequence. Every track plays exactly once before any repeats. No weighting, pure randomness.
ST — Stochastic
Weighted by Rating
Weighted random sampling using a log-scale rating distribution. Higher rated tracks are more likely to be picked. Unrated tracks default to 75.
EN — Entropy
Maximum Variety
Like Stochastic, but actively penalises recently played artists, genres and albums. Maximises variety across your listening session.
FC — Focus
Genre / Artist Focus
Steered toward a chosen artist, genre or album. Other tracks blend in when the focused pool runs low. Adjustable strength.
